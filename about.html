About Us

uOttawa Dentistry is a dental center located in Ottawa, Canada. It is a reputable dental practice that provides comprehensive oral health care services to patients of all ages. The center is affiliated with the University of Ottawa, ensuring that patients receive high-quality dental care backed by the latest advancements in dentistry.

At uOttawa Dentistry, a team of experienced and skilled dentists, hygienists, and dental professionals work together to deliver personalized and patient-centered dental care. They are committed to promoting oral health and providing treatments to address various dental needs, ranging from preventive care and routine check-ups to more specialized procedures.

The center offers a wide range of dental services, including dental cleanings, fillings, extractions, root canals, crowns, bridges, dentures, orthodontics, and cosmetic dentistry procedures. They utilize advanced dental technology and techniques to ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments.

The team at uOttawa Dentistry prioritizes patient comfort and strives to create a welcoming and relaxing environment. They understand the importance of patient education and take the time to explain treatment options, answer questions, and provide guidance for maintaining optimal oral health.

With a focus on continuous learning and professional development, uOttawa Dentistry stays up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in dentistry. This enables them to offer modern and evidence-based treatments, ensuring the best possible outcomes for their patients.

Overall, uOttawa Dentistry is dedicated to providing exceptional dental care in a compassionate and patient-centric manner. They aim to help individuals achieve and maintain healthy, beautiful smiles while promoting overall oral health and well-being.
