About Us

uOttawa Dentistry is a dental center located in Ottawa, Canada. It is a reputable dental practice that provides comprehensive oral health care services to patients of all ages. The center is affiliated with the University of Ottawa, ensuring that patients receive high-quality dental care backed by the latest advancements in dentistry.

Read More
Teeth Cleaning and Dental Check-ups:

We offer thorough dental examinations and teeth cleaning to maintain your oral health. Our experienced dentists and hygienists ensure that your teeth and gums are in optimal condition.

Restorative Dentistry:

If you have damaged or decayed teeth, our team can provide restorative dentistry services. This includes dental fillings, crowns, bridges, and implants to restore the function and appearance of your smile.

Cosmetic Dentistry:

Enhance your smile with our cosmetic dentistry options. We offer teeth whitening, dental veneers, bonding, and orthodontic treatments to help you achieve a confident and beautiful smile.
