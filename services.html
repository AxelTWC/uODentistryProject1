Services:

Teeth Cleaning and Dental Check-ups

One of the primary services offered at a dentist center is routine teeth cleaning and dental check-ups. These appointments involve a thorough examination of the teeth, gums, and mouth to assess oral health and detect any potential issues. The dentist or dental hygienist will clean and remove plaque and tartar buildup, polish the teeth, and provide guidance on proper oral hygiene practices.

Dr. Matthew White

Cost: 100 CAD Per Session

Restorative Dentistry

Another important service provided by a dentist center is restorative dentistry. This includes procedures aimed at repairing and restoring damaged or decayed teeth. Common restorative treatments may include dental fillings to treat cavities, dental crowns to restore and strengthen a tooth, and dental bridges or implants to replace missing teeth. The goal of restorative dentistry is to improve the functionality, aesthetics, and overall health of the patient's smile.

Dr. Olivia Stone

Cost: 500 CAD Per Session

Cosmetic Dentistry

Many dentist centers offer cosmetic dentistry services to enhance the appearance of a patient's smile. These services focus on improving the aesthetics of teeth and include procedures such as teeth whitening, dental veneers to address tooth imperfections, dental bonding to repair chipped or misshapen teeth, and orthodontic treatments like braces or clear aligners to straighten misaligned teeth. Cosmetic dentistry aims to create a more confident and attractive smile for patients.
